About Alex

Alex Bruce is a software developer, based in Grand Rapids, Michigan. He has been coding since he was ten. He co-founded 2Goo about a year ago, after working for some time on OnTask, the parent company of 2Goo. He is skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and C#.NET. In 2016, he won third place in the State of Michigan in a Python Programming competition. When he isn't coding, he likes to study video game plot and interface, study aviation, play Cello, and he is also a Fencer at the club in Grand Rapids.
